Welcome to the story of Alex Shapiro

The Beginning

The name's Alex, although my friends call me AJ. I was born in a small town
in Tennessee. Nobody great ever came out of that town. It was
broken, corrupt. No one could stop the mayor from buying off the police
whenever he needed to. That was where I first became a spy. No one was coming
to rescue us, so we started our own resistance. My family was on the wrong side.
The mayor was close with my father and even closer with my mother. At first,
I was blinded by my family and trusted them too much. After my eyes were
opened by an event that can only be refered to as The HairCut, I renounced
my family name and joined a resistance group by the name of Salvator.
Because they wronged me during The HairCut, my loyality strayed. At the
end of the Seven Day Assault, I destroyed Salvator from the inside and
appointed my own ruler, one who was just and kind-hearted. After
that, I left Tennessee and started my career.
