About Jason Allen


On Monday, March 29, 1999, at 3:03 AM, I was born. I came into the world in
Boston, Massachuesetts. I lived the first 18 years of my life in Norwood, Massachuesetts.
I went to Callahan Elementary School, the Philip O. Coakely Middle School, and then
Norwood High School. I graduated 10th in my class of 240 students and began my studies
at Northeastern University, where I am currently pursuing a degree in Computer Engineering
and computer science.
